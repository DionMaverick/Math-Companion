CIRCLE

It refers to a round-shaped figure that has no corners, edges, or sides.

DIAMETER

It refers to the length of the line that passes through the circle and connects two opposite points in the circle

RADIUS

It refers to the length of a line of a circle that goes from the center of the circle to any point on the circle

AREA OF A CIRCLE

It refers to the region occupied by circle in a two-dimensional plane.

ARC LENGTH

It refers to the portion of the circumference of the circle

CIRCUMFERENCE OF A CIRCLE

It refers to the perimeter of a circle or the linear distance around its edge.

PI

A mathematical constant roughly equal to 3.14, representing the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter.

AREA OF A SECTOR

It refers to the region occupied by the slice or the shaded portion in the circle

CENTRAL ANGLE

It refers to the measure of the "slice" of a circle.
