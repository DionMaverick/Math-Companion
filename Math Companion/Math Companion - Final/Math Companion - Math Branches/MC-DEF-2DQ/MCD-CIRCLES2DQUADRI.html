SQUARE

It refers to a four-sided polygon which is known as a quadrilateral with four equal sides.

SIDE LENGTH

It refers as the sides of a square it is the length and width of a square

PERIMETER OF A SQUARE

Refers to the total sum of the sides of square

AREA OF A SQUARE

Refers to the amount of space or surface covered by the square

DIAGONAL OF A SQUARE

Refers to the line segment that connects two opposite corners (vertices) of the square.

RECTANGLE

A rectangle is a four-sided shape with four right angles and opposite sides that are parallel and equal in length.

AREA OF A RECTANGLE

Refers to the amount of space or surface covered by the rectangle.

LENGTH

Longer side(s) of the rectangle.

WIDTH

Shorter side(s) of the rectangle, perpendicular to the length

PERIMETER OF A RECTANGLE

The perimeter is the sum of all its sides' lengths.
